Gift Booster Setup Guide

Get your gift campaign running in under 5 minutes!

Step 1: Install the App

Install Gift Booster from the Shopify App Store. The app will appear in your admin under "Apps".

Step 2: Set Your Threshold

Go to Settings and set your spending threshold. Popular amounts are $50, $75, or $100 depending on your average order value.

ðŸ’¡ Tip: Set your threshold 20-30% above your current average order value for best results.
Step 3: Choose Your Gift

Select a product from your store to use as the gift. Best choices are small, appealing items like:

  • Branded keychains or stickers
  • Sample products
  • Small accessories
  • Digital downloads
Step 4: Activate Your Campaign

Toggle the "Enable gift feature" switch to activate. Your gift campaign is now live!

Step 5: Monitor Results

Check the Analytics page regularly to track your performance and see how much additional revenue you're generating.

Best Practices

  • Choose gifts that complement your brand
  • Test different threshold amounts
  • Update gifts seasonally
  • Monitor analytics weekly
  • Promote your gift offer in marketing
