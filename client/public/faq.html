Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my first gift campaign?

It's simple! Go to Settings, set your spending threshold (e.g., $50), choose a product as your gift, and activate. The app will automatically add gifts when customers reach your threshold.

Can I use any product as a gift?

Yes! You can select any product from your store as a gift. Popular choices include keychains, stickers, samples, or small branded items.

How do I track my results?

Visit the Analytics page to see your performance metrics, including gifts added, conversion rates, and revenue impact.

Does this work with all themes?

Yes! Gift Booster works with all Shopify themes and is fully mobile responsive.

Can I set multiple gift thresholds?

Currently, you can set one gift threshold per campaign. Multiple thresholds are planned for future updates.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your Shopify admin under Apps > Gift Booster > Settings, or contact our support team.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial so you can test the app and see results before committing.

What if I need help with setup?

Contact our support team at support@giftbooster.app and we'll help you get set up quickly.
